Terms and Conditions

The words Terms & Conditions make us cringe too, but even the fabulous must follow a few rules.

1. First come first serve

First Mate rates are first come, first served and are based on ship capacity and may not be available on every sailing.

2. Eligibility

First Mate rates are only applicable to active First Mates who have completed the first three tiers of Seacademy - Violet, Scarlet, and Silver.

3. Call to book

You must call Sailor Services to book:

+1 (954) 488 2955 or

+44 (20) 30034919

4. Direct booking

The offer can only be applied through direct bookings.

5. Non-Combinable

All First Mate Rate bookings will be placed in the Essential fare class, with mandatory Service Gratuities applied. These rates are non-commissionable, non-refundable, and not eligible for voyage date changes or Future Voyage Credit. They are not combinable with Groups Benefits, Sea-Blazer eligibility or promotional offers (with the exception of Bar Tab).

6. Subject to availability

Cabins are subject to availability and a best available cabin will be offered at the time of booking.

7. Active First Mates

First Mates must be active at the time of sailing, must be the primary Sailor in the cabin, and can only bring one guest in the cabin (not applicable for 3rd and 4th guests in a cabin).

8. One cabin per sailing

Only one cabin may be booked per sailing date.

9. Paid in full

Reservation must be paid in full at the time of booking for all Sailors, including taxes and fees, and payment is non-refundable.

10. Cancellation

First Mate Rate bookings do not qualify for voyage date changes at any time and Sailors can not get Future Voyage Credit.

Other restrictions may apply

*Remember we have a no solicitation policy onboard, and we kindly ask that you don't discuss your discounted rate or promote your travel business to other Sailors while voyaging with us.
